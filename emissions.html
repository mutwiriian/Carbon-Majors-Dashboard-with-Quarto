The Carbon Majors database hosts data on emissions from 1854 by major producers of oil, gas, coal and cement. The data helps to track these production-linked emissions to the respective entities. It is self-reported by entities and can be used to hold corporations to account and in informing policy and strategies to minimize fossil fuel usage.
